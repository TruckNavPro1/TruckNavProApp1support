## 3. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

TruckNav Pro integrates with the following third-party services. Each service has its own privacy policy and data handling practices:

### 3.1 Supabase (Backend & Database)
**Purpose:** User authentication, data storage, and syncing
**Data Shared:** Email, user ID, truck settings, saved routes, trip history
**Privacy Policy:** https://supabase.com/privacy
**Data Location:** United States (configurable)

### 3.2 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)
**Purpose:** In-app purchase processing and subscription management
**Data Shared:** User ID, purchase receipts, subscription status
**Privacy Policy:** https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
**Note:** RevenueCat does NOT receive payment information (handled by Apple)

### 3.3 MapBox (Mapping & Navigation)
**Purpose:** Map display, geocoding, and navigation
**Data Shared:** Location data, map interactions, search queries
**Privacy Policy:** https://www.mapbox.com/legal/privacy
**Access Token:** Stored locally in the app

### 3.4 TomTom (Truck Routing)
**Purpose:** Truck-specific route calculation with restrictions
**Data Shared:** Origin/destination coordinates, vehicle dimensions, route preferences
**Privacy Policy:** https://www.tomtom.com/privacy/
**Note:** Routing calculations are performed via API; data is not permanently stored by TomTom

### 3.5 OpenWeather (Weather Data)
**Purpose:** Real-time weather information
**Data Shared:** Location coordinates for weather queries
**Privacy Policy:** https://openweathermap.org/privacy-policy

### 3.6 Apple (iOS Platform & Payments)
**Purpose:** App distribution, in-app purchases, device services
**Data Shared:** Apple ID, payment information (for subscriptions), device identifiers
**Privacy Policy:** https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

**IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER:** We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. You should review their privacy policies to understand how they handle your data.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Storage Locations
- **Local Storage:** Truck settings, preferences, and cached map data are stored on your device using UserDefaults and local file storage
- **Cloud Storage:** Account data, saved routes, and trip history are stored on Supabase servers (United States)
- **Third-Party Storage:** Data shared with MapBox, TomTom, and other services is subject to their retention policies

### 5.2 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication via Supabase Auth
- API key protection and access controls
- Regular security updates and patches

### 5.3 Data Retention
- **Location Data:** Active navigation sessions are stored temporarily; historical trip data is retained based on your subscription tier
- **Account Data:** Retained as long as your account is active
- **Usage Analytics:** Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

**IMPORTANT:** Despite our security efforts, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
